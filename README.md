## Hi, I'm Kristina 👋

I build products for people navigating systems that don't make sense to them — healthcare, HR, and now my own.

### What I'm building
**Mothering Together** — a postpartum support app with an AI companion, Elle, that helps new mothers through their first year. I researched it, designed it, and built it myself.
- 🔗 [Live product — request early access](https://motheringtogether.space)
- The production repo is private. It handles payment and health-adjacent data, so it stays closed while the product moves toward HIPAA-aligned infrastructure.

### What else I do
I run **fwd design co**, leading discovery and design for client work — focus groups, card sorts, stakeholder interviews, and the information architecture that comes out of them.
- 🔗 [fwddesign.co](https://fwddesign.co)

### Background
Six years owning enterprise HR systems for 1,500+ employees, three years running clinical information infrastructure at a hospital, and a Doctor of Science in Information and Interaction Design starting in 2026. I get fluent in unfamiliar systems fast, then use that fluency to ask better questions.
